Erika Perley
Full Stack Developer

What started out as a passion for hiring and working alongside developers at social startups fuelled an internal resounding voice that started as a whispering wave, why wasn't I 'programming' myself? My path as a developer quelled in the knowledge that I wasn't satisfied standing still. Jumping in with not much of a plan, going back to school as a second career was a brave and scary step, and it continues to be the mental equivalent of holding a good goofy stance on a solid board. The satisfaction of cresting a white wave, I'm proud that after a few short months, I am able to showcase my learnings through a site I've built myself. I look forward to surfing my next waves in exploring projects where these skills will be of value - on (and) off the beach.
